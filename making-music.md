# How to Structure Prompts for Suno AI

Creating effective Suno prompts is crucial for generating high-quality music.

**Example:**
    
    
    [Intro]
    *rain falling softly*
    [Verse 1]
    Walking through shadows,
    *whispers*
    ALL ALONE, yet the stars still shine bright
    [Chorus]
    WHISPER TO ME, tell me it's alright
    *thunder rumbles*
    DANCING IN THE DARK, hold on to the light
    [screaming vocals]
    Let the storm rage, we'll make it through the night

## Song structure[](<#song-structure>)

**1\. Define the Song’s Structure:** Use brackets to specify different sections of the song. Examples:

  * \[Intro\]
  * \[Verse\]
  * \[Chorus\]
  * \[Bridge\]



## Effects and dynamics[](<#effects-and-dynamics>)

**2\. Incorporate Effects and Dynamics:** Utilize asterisks for sound effects and capitalization for emphasis:

  * _soft wind sounds_
  * ALL CAPS for emphasis



## Styles and genres[](<#styles-and-genres>)

**3\. Specify Styles and Genres:** Refer to the **suno prompt list** to define the genre and style. Examples:

  * \[Jazz intro\]
  * \[Rock verse\]
  * \[Electronic beat\]



## Voice Techniques[](<#voice-techniques>)

**4\. Detail Vocal Techniques:** Guide the AI on vocal styles using descriptive prompts:

  * \[Whispering vocals\]
  * \[Screaming vocals\]



## Emotions and themes[](<#emotions-and-themes>)

**5\. Include Emotions and Themes:** Use emotive language to convey the song’s mood:

  * \[Haunting melody\]
  * \[Uplifting chorus\]
