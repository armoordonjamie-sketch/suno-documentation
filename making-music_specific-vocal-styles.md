# How to Get Specific Vocal Styles with Suno Prompts

When using Suno prompts to create music, you can guide the AI to generate specific vocal styles by providing detailed descriptions. Here are some tips to help refine your results: Key Strategies for Vocal Suno Prompts

## Vocal Characteristics:[](<#vocal-characteristics>)

Use terms like “\[deep masculine vocal\]” or “\[light feminine vocal\]” to specify tone and range.

## Stylistic Descriptions:[](<#stylistic-descriptions>)

Include stylistic cues like “\[airy\]” or “\[gritty\]” for added nuance.

## Regional Influences:[](<#regional-influences>)

Add regional styles, such as “\[British rock vocal\]” or “\[Southern gospel\]”, to tailor the AI’s vocal output.

Experiment and Refine your suno prompts

  * Try different combinations of vocal descriptors.
  * Save successful prompts for future use to build a reference library.



By experimenting and iterating, you’ll optimize your Suno prompts for more precise vocal outputs.
