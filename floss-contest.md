# Suno prompt inspiration for Flosstradamus contest

Suno is holding a remix contest with Flosstradamus, where participants can remix the track “Soundclash” using a Suno prompt. The best suno prompts will win prizes, with up to $25,000 for top entries. One lucky winner will also get the chance to perform live with Flosstradamus in New York. To enter, participants must create their remix with a Suno prompt, and winners will be selected based on likes, plays, and shares. The contest ends September 30, 2024. For more details, visit the [Suno blog](<https://suno.com/blog/floss>).

## Prompt after extending Flosstradamus’ stem[](<#prompt-after-extending-flosstradamus-stem>)

After clicking extend on one of Flosstradamus’ stems, combine the styles below with genres to get unique results from your suno prompts.

Ex. `Dubstep, Acid House, Haunting, Crisp, Layered`
