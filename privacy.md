# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: October 19, 2024_

At **HowToPromptSuno.com** \(“we”, “our”, “us”\), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1\. Information We Collect[](<#1-information-we-collect>)

When you log in to HowToPromptSuno.com via Google or use our services, we may collect the following types of data:

  * **Personal Information** : This includes your Google account information \(name, email address\) as well as IP addresses to prevent fraud and unauthorized access.
  * **Usage Data** : We collect information on how the Service is accessed and used \(e.g., analytics, session data, and interactions\). This includes data from third-party services like Google Analytics, FullStory, and Rudderstack.



We also use cookies \(see Section 6\) to enhance the user experience and analyze website usage.

## 2\. How We Use Your Information[](<#2-how-we-use-your-information>)

We use the data we collect for several purposes:

  * **Authentication** : To securely log you into our platform and prevent unauthorized access.
  * **Service Improvement** : We analyze user behavior to improve the functionality and quality of the Service.



We do **not** share your data with third-party companies or partners for marketing or advertising purposes.

## 3\. Data Storage and Security[](<#3-data-storage-and-security>)

We store your data on **Google Cloud** servers in the United States. We take the following steps to secure your data:

  * **Encryption** : We encrypt sensitive information both in transit and at rest.
  * **Secure Servers** : Our infrastructure uses industry-standard security measures to protect against data breaches.
  * **Access Control** : Only authorized personnel have access to personal data, and they are bound by confidentiality obligations.



## 4\. User Rights and Data Management[](<#4-user-rights-and-data-management>)

You have the right to:

  * **Access and Manage Your Data** : You can view, edit, or delete your account data through your account settings.
  * **Account Deletion** : You can delete your account at any time by navigating to the “Delete Account” option in settings.



Once you delete your account, your personal information will be removed from our systems within 30 days, and we retain no further access unless legally required.

## 5\. Data Retention[](<#5-data-retention>)

We retain personal information for **180 days** of inactivity. After this period, inactive accounts and their associated data will be deleted unless otherwise required by law.

## 6\. Cookies and Tracking Technologies[](<#6-cookies-and-tracking-technologies>)

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and store certain information. We use:

  * **Functional Cookies** : To remember your login information and preferences.
  * **Analytics Cookies** : To collect information on how you interact with our site.
  * **Advertising Cookies** : To provide personalized ads when applicable.



You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, if you choose to disable cookies, some parts of our Service may not function properly.

## 7\. International Data Transfers[](<#7-international-data-transfers>)

As we operate in the United States, all personal data collected from international users will be transferred to and stored in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer of data.

## 8\. Third-Party Services[](<#8-third-party-services>)

We use third-party services that may have access to your data to perform specific functions:

  * **Google** : For user authentication.
  * **Lemon Squeezy** : For payment processing and our merchant of record.



These third-party service providers only have access to personal data necessary to perform their functions and are required to protect it under similar privacy regulations.

## 9\. Minors[](<#9-minors>)

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 18. However, minors may use the Service. If you are aware of any data collected from minors without parental consent, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## 10\. Changes to This Privacy Policy[](<#10-changes-to-this-privacy-policy>)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any changes are made, we will notify you via email at least 15 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after the changes have been made will constitute your acknowledgment and acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
